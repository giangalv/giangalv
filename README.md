## Welcome to my GitHub! 🚀

I’m a robotics researcher at the University of Genoa, where I also earned my Master’s in Robotics Engineering, after completing my Bachelor’s in Industrial Automation Engineering at the University of Brescia.

My work is centered on mobile robotics, autonomous navigation, and learning-based control, with hands-on experience on quadrupeds like the Unitree Go2 and mobile manipulators such as the MiR250 + UR5e.

This GitHub is where I share projects, experiments, and ideas in robotics, automation, and programming, a space where theory meets real-world implementation. 🤖

If you’re passionate about intelligent machines, autonomous systems, and building technology that matters, you’re in the right place.

Let’s build the future together! 🌟
